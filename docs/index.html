##### Q. How did the internet start? * The Internet started in the 1960s as a way for government researchers to share information. * A reason in the formation of the Internet was the Cold War. * This eventually led to the formation of the ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network) for military use * ARPANET evolved into what we now know as the Internet. * The internet was fully established on January 1, 1983

The inventor of the world wide web, Tim Berners Lee

WWW refers to all the public websites or pages that users can access on their local computers

### Q. How was the world-wide-web invented? * Tim Berners-Lee, a British scientist, invented the World Wide Web (WWW) in 1989, while working at CERN. * CERN had scientists working at many different places so they needed a way to share documents easily. * info.cern.ch/ was the address of the world's first website which went live in November 1992. * Like other early websites, it has only **text** and **hypertext** * www.aliweb.com/ was launched in 1993, and was the first search engine.
### Q. What is text and hypertext? * A **text** is a passage of words that conveys a set of meanings to the person who is reading it. * **Hypertext** is text with hyperlinks to other text that the reader can immediately access.
### Activity for Lab * Open http://info.cern.ch/ and compare how it looks different to modern websites * Open http://www.aliweb.com/ and compare how it looks different to the first website.
##### Q. What are the components of a webpage? * **Headings:** a title at the head of a page or section * **Images:** pictures that were created or copied and stored in electronic form * **Links:** a connection between two webpages * **Logo:** images, texts, or shapes that depict the name and purpose of a business * **Menu:** a list of buttons or facilities displayed on screen. * **Text:** a passage of words
##### Q. How do you make a website? * Every website is based on HTML * HTML is a way to tell the browser to display headings, images, links, etc * HTML can be difficult to understand so we can also use a **web page editor** like wix.com * These web page editors allow you to add components directly and they will generate the HTML for you
